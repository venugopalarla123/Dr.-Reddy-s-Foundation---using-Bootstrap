Dr. Reddy’s Laboratories & Its Visionary Founder

Dr. Reddy’s Laboratories, one of India’s leading pharmaceutical companies, was founded in 1984 by the visionary Dr. K. Anji Reddy. Known for his groundbreaking contributions to the pharmaceutical industry, Dr. Reddy was committed to making high-quality and affordable medicines accessible to millions across the globe. His pioneering efforts transformed the healthcare landscape in India, and his legacy continues to inspire innovation and excellence in the industry.

Beyond his professional achievements, Dr. Reddy was deeply passionate about social impact, which led to the establishment of Dr. Reddy’s Foundation (DRF) in 1996. Guided by his belief that true success lies in uplifting the underserved, the foundation focuses on education, skill-building, and livelihood generation for marginalized communities. By blending compassion with innovation, DRF has empowered countless individuals to break free from poverty and achieve economic independence.

Dr. K. Anji Reddy’s life exemplified the spirit of giving back to society. While his pharmaceutical enterprise revolutionized access to medicine, his foundation continues to shape lives and create sustainable change, cementing his legacy as a pioneer in both industry and philanthropy.

Empowering the Future with Technology

At Dr. Reddy’s Foundation, we believe that everyone deserves an opportunity to succeed in today’s digital world. To make this vision a reality, we offer free training programs in cutting-edge fields like Full Stack Development and Data Science, ensuring that individuals from underserved communities can access world-class education and career opportunities.

Full Stack Development Program

Our Full Stack Development course is designed to transform students into skilled software developers proficient in both front-end and back-end technologies. From mastering programming languages like JavaScript, Python, and React to building dynamic web applications and APIs, learners gain hands-on experience with the tools and frameworks in demand by top companies.

Data Science Program

The Data Science program prepares students to excel in one of the fastest-growing and most lucrative career fields. Students learn to analyze, visualize, and interpret complex data using tools like Python, R, SQL, and Tableau. The course also covers advanced topics such as Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence, empowering learners to create predictive models and data-driven solutions.
